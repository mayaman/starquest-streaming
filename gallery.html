☆ StarQuest Private Preview ☆

Solo exhibition by Maya Man
Presented by Feral File
Opens to the public on November 20, 2025
Curated by Nora O’ Murchú

StarQuest by Maya Man turns to the world of American competition dance—its sequins, discipline, and ambition—to consider how we perform, compete, and live through the unreal realities of AI.

Built from a dataset of 111 AI-generated dance scenes, each eight seconds long, StarQuest turns data into drama—where performance is coded, rehearsed, and replayed. Drawing on the emotion and spectacle of the cult reality series Dance Moms, and on her own background, Man transforms the world of competition dance into a meditation on visibility, labor, and control.

Collect three or more scenes, and a generative episode powered by Man’s custom-built player automatically emerges from the scenes held at a single on-chain address.

Generative episodes are currently available to reserve as a set.
